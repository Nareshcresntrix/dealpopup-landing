HOW IT WORKS

From sign-up to first redemption in four steps

Create your profile

Add your brand, branches and contact details. We verify every merchant, so shoppers know you're the real thing.

Deals, coupon codes or full weekly catalogs. Set prices in AED, pick your emirates, choose start and end dates.
